WHO AM I?

I'm Chitundu Milimbo!
A Zambian based in Lusaka, I'm fluent in English but i can speak Nyanja and Bemba and I learned a little bit of French when I was younger. I was born in Lusaka in 2001, lived in Lusaka my whole life. I have always loved the idea of working in the tech industry so I didnt have much to think about when it got to that point, I believe I also have the creativity that makes this industry what it is.

EDUCATION
